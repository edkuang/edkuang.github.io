Gentlemen, behold! The projects i made in ame!

word design

This is my design of the word "quiet". This was one of the first projects I made and a big obstacle. In order to represent the word quiet, I created space. I chose to create space because I felt like the blank space is empty which is quiet. The design is in the sky because the sky looks really blank and quiet.

park sign project

This is the project that I consider my first major adobe illustrator project. It was challenging at first, but I eventually got better at it and I can see myself doing this again easily. I followed a tutorial on this one. I learned about creating color swatches and different ways to adjust objects.

3d text art

I worked really hard and came short of an A grade. This was an extra credit opportunity I took for the grade boost that got me to an A. I stayed afterschool working on this. I followed a tutorial on how to make 3D text art. I learned about different special effects and editing specific parts of an object.

My favorite project

Social Justice Poster

Animal Injustice

I made a social justice poster for AME. I chose the topic animal injustice because I believe animals are treated horribly by us humans. We need to speak for the animals. This project was inspired by other animal rights posters. This project was a bunch of trial and errors with effects. I tried to make the poster have a lot of detail while being simple. Overall I am happy with the project. The thing in the middle is supposed to be a collar. I tried to create contrast between the two images.
